### ✨ AI Assistant Advisor

<Frame>
 <img
 src="/resources/tqKbqf0Ik45wszeQDfvWBYCWj4XiBXPe8RGJFU65.jpg"
 alt="Image"
 />
</Frame>

Your AI assistants now have their own personal optimization coach. The new **AI Advisor** automatically analyzes your assistant's configuration and call performance, then gives you clear recommendations to improve call quality — with one-click fixes.

Open any assistant and you'll see a smart banner telling you exactly how many settings could be improved. Click **"View &amp; fix"** to open the full advisor panel.

### 💡 How It Works
The advisor runs **41 intelligent rules** across three categories:

- **Recommendations** — Settings that aren't optimal for your use case. Things like voice speed, temperature values, silence thresholds, and endpoint sensitivity. Each recommendation explains *why* it matters and what to change.
- **Troubleshoot** — Experiencing issues on calls? Echo, interruptions, the AI not following your prompt, calls cutting off too early? Select the problem and the advisor shows exactly which settings to adjust.
- **Best Settings** — A curated guide to the optimal configuration for each voice mode (Pipeline, Multimodal, and Dualplex).

### 🔧 One-Click Fix
Every recommendation comes with a **"Fix"** button that automatically applies the optimal value — no manual editing needed. You can also hit **"Fix All"** to apply every recommendation at once. The advisor updates in real time as you make changes.

<Update label="V1.7.2" description="Jan 26 2026">

### 🔥 New Call Features: Warm Transfers + Keypad Digits Collection

We’ve added two major capabilities to make AI phone agents more reliable in real-world support and sales scenarios — smoother human handoff and faster, more accurate data capture.

☎️ Warm Call Transfer (Hold + Brief + Connect)

<Frame>
 <img
 src="/resources/SrwBrXtF1hUPX4WDd53NM7Rfh0YxrhP0NFewY5AU.png"
 alt="Image"
 />
</Frame>

When a caller asks to speak with a human, your AI can now run a **true warm transfer**: it puts the customer on hold (with music or silence), dials a supervisor/agent, **briefs them with context**, and only then connects both sides.

**Why it’s better**

- ✅ **Context preserved:** agents don’t start cold — they already know what the customer needs.
- 🎶 **Professional experience:** caller hears a hold message + hold music while the agent is reached.
- 🔁 **More compatible transfers:****Web calls can now transfer to real human phone agents** (the initial interaction doesn’t need to be a phone call anymore).
- For **SIP trunks without SIP REFER**, warm transfer now works by creating a new **SIP INVITE** and merging calls — no REFER required.

**Configurable options** Supervisor number, caller ID mode, hold music/silence, hold message, supervisor briefing instructions, and the final “connected” message.

⌨️ Customer Keypad Digits Collection (DTMF)

<Frame>
 <img
 src="/resources/0R4hEUj2GrQ4btiOSJRqOqD22k7ZE87dhYECpzru.png"
 alt="Image"
 />
</Frame>

Customers can now enter digits on their phone keypad during a conversation, and the assistant receives the input instantly and continues naturally.

**How it works**

- Runs **in the background** — no awkward pauses
- Auto-submits after a configurable timeout (default **5s**)
- Instant submit when the user presses **#** or *****
- Works alongside voice: customers can **speak or type**

**Perfect for** Account numbers, PINs, phone numbers, license plates, order codes — especially when accuracy and privacy matter.

**Configurable options** Input timeout + submit key (# or *).

These upgrades make your assistants feel more human, more reliable, and easier to deploy in production support environments.

</Update>

<Update label="v1.4.8" description="Aug 14 2025">

### 🧠 Campaigns just got smarter

We’ve added powerful new controls to help you get more out of your outbound campaigns, avoid wasted calls, and make sure every lead gets the attention it deserves.

Here’s what’s new:

### **1. Campaign Call Status Options**
You now have **three new controls** at the campaign level to fine-tune how your leads are called:

- **Retry until goal completed** *Continue calling until the goal is achieved.* Perfect for situations where a lead answers but doesn’t complete the desired action (e.g., no appointment booked). Calls will continue until the selected **goal variable** is marked as true.
- **Retry on voicemail** *Continue calling until a human answers (skip voicemails).* If a call hits voicemail, the lead won’t be marked as completed — the system will retry until someone picks up.
- **Goal variable** *Variable that must be true to mark lead as completed.* Select from your variables which one determines a “completed” lead. If the variable is false, the lead will be retried instead of being marked as completed.

### **2. Update Lead Data/Status from Automation Platform or API**
You can now **update lead details and statuses directly from your connected automation tools or API calls**.

- Change lead status (e.g., “created” instead of “completed”) dynamically.
- Push updated information into your campaigns without manual edits.

### **3. Ringing Timeout in Assistant Settings**
A new **ringing timeout** option in the Assistant settings lets you control how long to wait for an answer before moving on.

- For outbound campaigns, set it **lower** to avoid voicemail and save agent time.
- For campaigns with slower-answering customers, increase it to give them more time to pick up.

</Update>
