# WhatTalk.ai

Automate phone calls with AI. Welcome to the WhatTalk.ai documentation hub. Whether you're a product owner, marketer, or developer, these guides will help you build, launch, and scale AI-powered voice experiences.

<Note>
  Need help getting started? Jump to our [core concepts guide](/getting-started/core-concepts) or [contact support](https://whattalk.ai/contact).
</Note>

## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up on WhatTalk.ai">
    Create your free account and access your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first AI assistant">
    Define the prompt, choose a voice, and connect any tools you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start making & receiving calls">
    Attach a phone number, launch a campaign, or route inbound calls to your assistant.
  </Step>
</Steps>
